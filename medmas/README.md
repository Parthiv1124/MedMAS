MedMAS is a research-validated multi-agent AI health platform built for rural and underserved India. Unlike single-model health apps that failed at scale, MedMAS deploys 6 specialist AI agents coordinated by a central orchestrator — covering symptom triage, chronic disease prediction, mental health support, lifestyle scoring, multilingual access, and frontline worker assistance. Every response is delivered in the user's native language, grounded in ICMR Indian clinical guidelines, and designed so AI assists — never replaces — the doctor.
